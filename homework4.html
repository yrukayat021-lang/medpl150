Reflection

My photomontage explores the political and economic conflict occurring in Nigeria. The message of my piece is to show the difficulties Nigerians go through on a daily basis. From bad roads, to lack of transportations, poor hospitals and medical care. Increased pollution is one of the consequences of overconsumption in Europe. Consumption does not end at the checkout counter, it continues in the lands and lives of others. I incorporated an image of workers cleaning a hole that seems to be in an area where people are living. This clearly depicts the polluted land in Nigeria. I tried to use layer masks to seamlessly blend the landfill into the lower half of the texture image. To provide an image easy on the eyes.
