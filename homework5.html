Illustrated Environment

I chose this environmental setting because the different types of trees and perspective allowed me to experiment with color just a little, and it allowed me to form a simple vector illustration. Using Adobe Illustrator, I placed the photograph on its own locked layer and used it as a template while recreating the environment with vector shapes and paths to the best of my ability. I divided the illustration into foreground, middle ground, and background layers to maintain depth and provide the viewers with contrast between them.

I manually redrew the environmental features using tools such as the Pen Tool. This approach allowed me to transform the photo environment into a simplified vector style creation while still preserving the atmosphere of the original photo.
