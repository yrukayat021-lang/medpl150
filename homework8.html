Homework #8a: Interactive Story Assignment, Part 1

Story description

This interactive story is of a college student on their first day of school. The story takes place in Hunter College where we’ll see how the decisions of the user affects the students' performance. Throughout the story, the user chooses how the new student reacts to new situations, such as entering/finding classrooms, interacting with other students etc. The story includes 2 paths. One path is of a happy ending the other is of a sad ending.
