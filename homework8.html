First Day of School

You are about to enter school for the first time. You feel nervous.This interactive story is of a college student on their first day of school. The story takes place in Hunter College where we’ll see how the decisions of the user affects the students' performance. Throughout the story, the user chooses how the new student reacts to new situations, such as entering/finding classrooms, interacting with other students, The story includes 2 paths. One path is of a happy ending the other is of a sad ending.
